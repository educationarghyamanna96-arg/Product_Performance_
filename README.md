# Financial & Product Performance Dashboard

An interactive data visualization dashboard analyzing revenue, inventory capital, and product performance metrics across multiple locations and categories (Cosmetics, Haircare, Skincare).

## 📊 Dashboard Overview


### 🔑 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* **Total Revenue:** ₹577.60K
* **Total Units Sold:** 46K
* **Total Capital in Inventory:** ₹243.86K
* **Average Defect Rate:** 2.28%

---

## 🔍 Key Insights & Visualizations

### 1. Revenue Breakdown (Donut Chart)
* Evaluates the share of total revenue contributed by product categories. 
* **Skincare** holds the largest market share, closely followed by **Cosmetics** and **Haircare**.

### 2. Geographic Performance (Bar Chart)
* Ranks revenue generation by major regional hubs.
* **Mumbai** and **Kolkata** lead as top-performing locations (generating > ₹100K each), followed by **Chennai**, **Bangalore**, and **Delhi**.

### 3. Capital in Inventory by Product Type & SKU (Treemap)
* Offers a granular view of frozen capital allocated across individual Stock Keeping Units (SKUs).
* **Cosmetics (Blue)** and **Haircare (Orange)** occupy the largest blocks of inventory capital compared to **Skincare (Dark Blue)**, highlighting potential overstocking or high-value items in those specific segments.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Tools Used
* **Business Intelligence Tool:** Power BI 
* **Data Sources:** Supply chain sales data, inventory logs, regional logistics records.
* **Design/Theme:** Custom Dark Mode theme with high-contrast accents (Gold/Neon).

## 🚀 How to View the Interactive Dashboard
1. Download the source file from this repository.
2. Open it using the corresponding desktop application.
3. Use the left-hand **Product** and **Location** slicers to dynamically filter the data.
